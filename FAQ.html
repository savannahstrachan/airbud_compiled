Frequently Asked Questions

What is AirBud?

We are a platform through which humans can make their dogs available for other humans who, for whatever reason, can't be full-time dog parents. Our users are people with full-time jobs who can't be home during the day to give their dogs the attention or exercise they need. They're people with small apartments who can't have dogs. They're people who aren't sure about what breed is right for them and want to test drive a few models!

What are the member requirements?

All members with profiles need to be over 18 years old and be the actual owner of their dogs. We won't accept members with a history of animal abuse.
All dogs must be up to date with vaccines, and cannot have a bite record.

Where do we meet?

The world is your playground! AirBud users meet in any environment where the dog is most comfortable:

  • The local dog park
  • A walk around the city
  • In the dog-proof home of either human.

Are there fees?

Not at all! This is a user-based exchange. We facilitate the meeting of dogs and humans and that's it! Spreading the joy of dogs is what we want to do, and we don't think you should have to spend money to do it.

On the main page, we do have a donate button, as well as links to the SPCA. If you like what we're doing here, or want to support your local animal shelter, you should feel free to give!
